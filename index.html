When I became Catholic I spent a lot of time looking for public domain Catholic books. One of the things that attracted me strongly to Catholicism was the amazing history. But despite so many Catholic works being in the public domain and available without cost, they are rarely put together as a well curated collection. This means that hours can be spent searching for a specific book, only to find that the available version (usually archive.org) is so riddled with errors that it is functionally not readable. In response to this experience I decided to put together my own curated collection of Catholic classics and save a lot of time for someone making the same search that I was.
